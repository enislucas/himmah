Two short, optional guides. Do them only if you want the extras.

Himmah works fully without any of this. When you first open it, a guided tour walks you through everything, and ✦ AI → ☢ Make it yours will build your tasks, habits and rhythms from a quick chat. The guides below just switch on the two optional online features.
1 · Switch on the AI
Smart triage, "Calendarize tomorrow", ✦ Enhance, the preferences quiz, and "Make it yours". You add your own DeepSeek key; a hard 10¢/day cap keeps it cheap.
~2 MIN · NEEDS A DEEPSEEK KEY
2 · Google Meet links
Make the 🎥 button on a calendar event create a real Google Meet link. Fiddly Google permissions, done once. (You can also just paste a link you already have with 🔗 - no setup.)
~10 MIN · OPTIONAL · FREE
